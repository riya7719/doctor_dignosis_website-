What is DxGPT?

DxGPT is an advanced AI-assisted medical diagnosis platform developed by Foundation29. We use advanced language
models to provide rapid differential analysis from symptom descriptions and clinical histories.

The goal is to enhance‚Äînot replace‚Äîclinical judgment: save time, neutralize cognitive biases, and raise the quality of clinical
reasoning from the first moment. Key features: intelligent differential diagnosis
with prioritized lists of possible diagnoses; multilingual support with automatic language detection and result translation; GDPR-ready architecture with automatic
anonymization of sensitive data; real-time asynchronous communication for advanced processing models.


How does DxGPT work?

DxGPT works in 4 simple steps:

1. Describe your symptoms by entering a medical description in free text.

2. Intelligent processing: Our AI algorithms analyze the information using advanced language models, detecting clinical
patterns and medical correlations.

3. Differential diagnosis: You receive a prioritized list of possible diagnoses, each with information about compatible symptoms,
recommended diagnostic tests, and detailed case analysis.

4. Interactive refinement: You can answer AI-generated follow-up questions to refine the diagnosis, obtain detailed
information about specific diseases, or request genetic testing recommendations.

Powered by cutting-edge artificial intelligence models and secure cloud infrastructure on Microsoft Azure.
